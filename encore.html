Sowmya Medabalimi presents a Greek play by Sophocles.

Philoctetes by Sophocles

Philoctetes

_Philoctetes pauses, then speaks with intensity._

What assurance have I that thy words bear truth? Shall I believe the one who once betrayed me?

Neoptolemus

I understand thy doubt, and justly so. Yet hear me now: my heart is torn with guilt.

Philoctetes

_Philoctetes shakes his head, a mixture of anger and sorrow._

Guilt alone cannot erase the pain I've suffered. Speak not of remorse unless thy actions prove it.
